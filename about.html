Introduction

Azibuyemasisweni Tourism Consulting Agency is a registered business dealing with:

  • Community Development Tourism
  • Tourism Awareness
  • Product Development
  • Tourism Workshops
  • Youth Development Programs
  • Entrepreneurship Programs
  • Unemployed Graduates Placement Programs

We are CATHSETA Accredited and Nationally Accredited as Tourism Specialists in South Africa.

The Plan

We develop and deliver employment readiness workshops to engage, and equip unemployed graduates around South Africa with the necessary skills they will need going into the corporate world. This transformative program will include interactive exercises, practical examples, and real time coaching in a fun yet focused environment.
